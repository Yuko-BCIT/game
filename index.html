How to play

High Low is a number guessing game.
Guess the number randomly generated by computer. There are 3 difficulty levels to choose from -

  • Easy (range 1~100)
  • Medium (1~500)
  • Hard (1~1000)
You have 10 lives and you'll get a hint for every number entry. Guess the number before you run out of lives!
